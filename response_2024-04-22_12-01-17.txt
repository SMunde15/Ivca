## Getting Your Mail for Your Residence

To best help you with your mail, I need some additional information. Here are a few options depending on your situation:

**1. If you're moving to a new residence:**

* **Forward your mail:** Set up mail forwarding with the post office to have your mail sent to your new address. This is crucial to avoid missing important letters or bills. You can do this online or by visiting your local post office.
* **Update your address:** Inform important institutions and individuals about your new address. This includes your bank, employer, subscriptions, and anyone else who sends you mail regularly.
